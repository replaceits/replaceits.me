Kana Totsugeki
Kana Totsugeki (仮名突撃) is a web game designed to help you learn or test your Japanese kana! Kana is a bit like the japanese alphabet, it consists of both hiragana (ひらがな) and katakana (カタカナ). Each round you need to enter in the right romaji (romanization of the japanese kana), hiragana, or katakana of the character we give you. If you get it right, you get 100 points! Get it wrong however and you will loose 100 points and a life. You have 3 lives but are able to choose between 1, 3, or 5 in the options menu. The options menu will also let you decide what kind of questions and answers will show up! If you start to get the hang of it, you can change the speed to be faster! Or if you aren't quite hitting the mark, slow it down!
